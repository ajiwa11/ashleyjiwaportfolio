Elephant Training Programs

Basic Obedience Training
Foundation training for young elephants or new arrivals, focusing on behavior management and building a positive relationship with handlers.

Behavior Modification
Addressing challenging behaviors through ethical, positive reinforcement methods. Our approach ensures the mental and emotional well-being of the elephant while promoting long-term behavior change.

Advanced Training & Enrichment
For elephants that are already trained, we offer advanced behavioral training, including enrichment programs to stimulate cognitive abilities and maintain physical health.

Health & Safety Education
Educating handlers and caretakers on proper elephant health care, safety protocols, and how to respond to medical emergencies.

Consulting for Zoos & Sanctuaries
Providing expert advice and hands-on training to zoos, sanctuaries, and conservation programs to help maintain the highest standards of care and behavior management for elephants.
